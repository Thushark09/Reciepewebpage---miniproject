Chicken Kabab

Authentic homemade chicken kabab recipe

Chicken Kabab is a delicious fried chicken dish made with spices and marinated chicken.

Preparation Time

  • Preparation Time - 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time - 20 minutes

Serves: 4 people

Ingredients

  • Chicken - 2 kg
  • Eggs - 2
  • Salt - 1 tablespoon
  • Turmeric - 1 teaspoon
  • Ginger garlic paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Chilli powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Garam masala - 1 tablespoon
  • Lemon - 1
  • Dhaniya powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Jeera powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Onions - 1
  • Fried curry leaves - 1 tablespoon

Cooking Steps

  1. Take a bowl and add the following ingredients:
    • Chicken
    • Eggs
    • Salt
    • Turmeric
    • Ginger garlic paste
    • Chilli powder
  2. Keep the chicken aside for 10 minutes for marination
  3. Take a deep frying pan and pour 2 glasses of oil
  4. Fry the marinated chicken until the chicken turns golden brown
  5. Serve the chicken in a plate and garnish the chicken with onions and fried Curry leaves

Tip: Marinate the chicken for longer time for better taste.
